about

I'm Katie and I'm a product manager, instructor and front-end developer.

I'm a natural problem solver who isn't afraid of a challenge and am a firm believer that being able to laugh at yourself makes any project possible.

I'm a "big picture"​ thinker who is able to see problems before they happen, leading to polished projects in education and community outreach. Building educational experiences is my specialty, but working with people for people is what I do best. I believe in working towards greater digital access for all, the passion and dedication of small teams, the unlimited potential of technology, and the power of a good cup of coffee.
